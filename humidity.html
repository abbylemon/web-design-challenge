Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

There is no trend between humidity and latitude of a city. But this plot does show that the city with the lowest humidity is close to the equator. Since deserts are more likly to be near the equator, this makes sense. Another check that this code is working properly is that there are not any cities with a humidity greater than 100%.

An area that could be further investigated is why there is a dip in the humidity around the -40 degrees latitude cities. It is possible that there is a large desert in that region.
