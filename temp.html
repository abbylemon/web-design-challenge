Summary: Latitude vs. Temp

There seems to be a clear trend between the maximum temperature of a city and it's corresponding latitude. The peak can be found at 0 degrees latitude, which is expected. What is more suprising, is the fact that this trend is not even. For example, the 60 degrees latitude cities have a max temperature of -40C where the -60 degrees latitude cities have a max temperature of 10 C. This is most likly due to ocianic effects on the temperature.

Another interesting aspect of this plot, is that there seems to be more deviation in the max temperature of the cities the further away from the equator you go. Whether this deviation change is truly significiat, is something that could be further investigated.
