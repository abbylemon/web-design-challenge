Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed

There is no trend between wind speed and latitude of cities. There does seem to be a cluster of data below 5 mph. There are noticable spikes in the wind speed the further away from the equator the cities are. Could this have something to do with the magnetic poles? Further investigation would need to be done to validate that the magnetic poles effected the wind speed.
